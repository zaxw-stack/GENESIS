About Us

Milele Salon & Barbershop is a friendly neighborhood beauty spot in Nairobi, known for stylish cuts, neat beard grooming, and trusted hair-care recommendations.

  • Professional hairdressing and barber services for men, women, and kids.
  • Premium styling products for natural hair, braids, and everyday care.
  • A warm, welcoming experience right here in Nairobi.

Whether you need a fresh trim, a detailed shave, or the right product for your hair routine, our team is ready to help you look and feel your best. We are proud to serve clients from across Nairobi with care and consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are simple answers to the most common questions about our salon, barbershop, and haircare services.

Do you accept walk-in clients?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins. If you want a specific time, booking ahead is a good idea, especially on busy days.

Do you offer services for men and women?

Yes. We offer haircuts, styling, beard grooming, and treatment services for both men and women.

Can I buy hair products from your salon?

Yes. We sell trusted hair products such as shampoos, oils, creams, and styling items to help you care for your hair at home.

How long does a haircut usually take?

A regular haircut usually takes around 45 minutes to 1 hour. Treatments and detailed styling may take a little longer.

Do you offer services for special occasions?

Yes. We help clients get ready for events, interviews, weddings, and other special occasions with polished styling and finishing touches.

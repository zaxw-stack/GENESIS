Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about sik0 tech's services and processes

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Most MVPs take 8-12 weeks with our 2-week sprint model. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and maintain regular communication to ensure timely delivery.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with modern tech stacks including React, Node.js, Python, Go, cloud platforms like AWS and GCP, and emerging technologies like AI/ML. Our team stays updated with the latest industry trends and best practices.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! We provide comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, feature enhancements, performance optimization, and security updates. Support levels range from business hours to 24/7 managed services depending on your needs.

How do you ensure code quality?

We follow strict development standards including test-driven development, automated testing, code reviews, continuous integration, and performance monitoring. Every line of code undergoes rigorous quality checks before deployment.

What is your communication process?

We maintain transparent communication through weekly sprint meetings, daily standups, and regular demos. You'll have direct access to your project team and receive detailed progress reports. Agile methodology ensures your feedback is incorporated continuously.
